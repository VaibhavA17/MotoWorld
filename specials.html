Wheels On White Overview

Highlights of Wheels On White Tour:


Adore the stunning view of Kinner Kailash peaks over a cup of tea.
Experience in a local Spitian homestay.

Freezing Temperature Below 0 Degrees

About the Places:

Nako Village:
Highest village in the valley, Nako owns a lake formed out of the masses of the ice. Fauna including, Yaks, kine, horses and asses, can be found in abundance here.
Spiti:
Spiti is one of the coldest places in the country. Snuggled at an altitude of 12,500 feet above the sea level, the valley serves as a paradise to adventure lovers.
Key Monastery:
A famous Tibetan Buddhist monastery, Key Monastery is also known as the Kye Monastery and Ki Monastery. Explore the long and interesting history of the monastery with this tour.
Kalpa:
Having beautiful temples and monasteries, the village of Kalpa is famous for its apple orchards.

About the Wheels On White Tour:


Get ready to start the adventure from Chandigarh. and go to all the places you wished to visit. This tour allows with all the experiences where you get to experience the beauty of winter and have memories that you can share with all.
Drive through places like Kufri, Narkanda and many more. Get to explore Chitkul. Chitkul is the last village on the old Hindustan - Tibet Trade route. It is on the banks of river Baspa and is the last place Indians can visit without a permit. Situated at an altitude of 2759 m from the sea level, Kalpa is located close to the foot of Kinner Kailash, the winter home of Lord Shiva.
Spiti Valley is a paradise to all the trekkers and mecca to travel lovers. Spiti means 'The Middle Land' and it separates India from Tibet. It is the best place to visit to experience the winter Spiti as Spiti is one of the coldest places in the country. Sprawling 360-degree views of the beautiful mountains will take your heart away.
All in all the tour is best suited to go for a perfect trip to explore Spiti in winter and allows you to create memories that are worth-sharing and worth-living. The Spiti Valley In Winter tour will be remembered for the rest of your life and you will have stunning pictures for your social media.

Wheels On White Itinerary (Changable According To The Weather And condition)


Day 1 - Departure from Chandigarh

Get ready to be departed from Chandigarh at around 8:00 pm.
The Spiti quest starts from here.

Day 2 - Reaching Chitkul | Reach Shimla and Drive Through Kufri

Reach Shimla next morning
Have your breakfast, freshen up and drive through Kufri, with Narkanda on the way to Sangla valley.
You will be reaching Chitkul by evening.
After dinner, go for a good night's sleep.

Day 3 - Transfer from Chitkul to Kalpa | Experience the White Wonderland

After breakfast, spend some time at leisure and check out.
Chitkul is the last Indian village towards China border. Explore the best of it.
Next, you will be departing for Kalpa.
After reaching Kapla, by evening, check-in to your hotels and have dinner.
Call it a day off and go for sleep for winter Spiti.

Day 4 - Start your journey to Spiti Valley - Kaza | Visit Nako Tabo villages en route

Wake up early in the morning and start your journey towards Spiti valley after breakfast.
Visit Nako Tabo villages en route Kaza as you travel through Sumdo Border.
By evening, you will be reaching Kaza and checking in to your hotel/camp.
After dinner, go to sleep.

Day 5 - Spiti Valley Sightseeing | View Key Monastery

Divine Spiti Valley sightseeing awaits you after having breakfast.
Pay a visit to the Key Monastery.
Come back to Kaza.
Retire for the day.

Day 6 - Chicham Bridge | Kibber

On this day of Spiti Valley In Winter Tour, you get to explore Chincham Bridge after having breakfast.
Chincham Bridge is the highest bridge in Asia and allows you to have panoramic views.
No to mention, famous Snow Leopard Spotting village, Kibber to be visited as well.
Come back to Kaza and relish your dinner.
Enjoy your sleep.

Day 7 - Transfer to Kalpa | Relax with serene surrounding
Check out from the hotel after having breakfast.
Get ready to depart for Kalpa and reach by night.
Have dinner at the hotel you check-in to and go to sleep.

Day 8 - Reaching Shimla | Departure

Wake up early morning and after breakfast depart for Shimla.
Reach Shimla by evening.
Depart for Chandigarh.
Day 9 - Chandigarh Ariival

Arrive in Chandigarh in the morning of day 9.
The Spiti Valley winter tour package ends with a lot of memories.
